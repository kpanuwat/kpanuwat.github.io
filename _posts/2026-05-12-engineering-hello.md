## Why

Keeping scattered notes in my head doesn't scale. This hub is a forcing function: if I spent time understanding something, write it down.

Engineering posts will cover systems I build, tools I adopt or reject, debugging patterns, and architecture decisions — the kind of detail that's obvious at the time and forgotten six months later.

## What to expect

- Short posts over long ones. Bias toward the "why" and the non-obvious "how".
- Real configs, real commands. No sanitised toy examples.
- Topics: CLI tools, distributed systems, infra, language-level internals, agent tooling.
